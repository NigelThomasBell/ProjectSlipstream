'''Cars''' in ''[[Forza Motorsport 7]]'' can be acquired from the Buy Cars menu, [[Specialty Dealer]], [[Auction House]] or from Driver Level rewards. It is also possible to earn cars from the [[Forza Driver's Cup]] and [[Forza Motorsport 7/Forzathon|Forzathon events]].

Different to previous titles, each car is assigned to a [[Car Collection|Car Collection tier]] along with a score that will be added to the player's overall collection score upon purchase or receiving the car as a reward. Players gain a higher tier level for collecting a certain amount of points, which will allow them to unlock more cars.

All cars that were obtained outside the Buy Cars menu are homologated, meaning their performance is already optimized for competition within their division.

Since the July 2018 update, all cars except for [[Unicorn]]s and Forza Edition cars have been made available for purchase from the Buy Cars menu.

Similar to [[Horizon Edition|Horizon Edition cars]] in ''[[Forza Horizon 3]]'', the title features Forza Edition cars that increase the player's credits rewards. Each car also comes with performance upgrades and bespoke visual appearances as standard.

'''Cars''' in ''[[Forza Horizon 4]]'' are purchaseable from the [[Autoshow]], [[Forza Horizon 4/Forzathon Shop|Forzathon Shop]] and [[Auction House]]. Additional cars for purchase may be acquired through [[Forza Horizon 4/Downloadable Content|downloadable content]].

==Synopsis==
A series of cars cannot be obtained directly and are rewarded to players in different manners including [[Forza Horizon 4/Barn Find|Barn Find]]s, [[Forza_Horizon_4/Cars/Wheelspin|Wheelspin cars]], [[:Category:Horizon Life Reward (FH4)|Horizon Life rewards]], [[Forza Horizon 4/Player House|Player House rewards]], [[Forza Horizon 4/Festival Playlist|Festival Playlist]]-only cars that may be made unlockable again in a future Season, and the [[Forza_Horizon_4/Horizon_Backstage|Horizon Backstage]].

There are also special car variants in the game including [[Forza Horizon 4/Preorder Car|Preorder Cars]] that come with bespoke upgrade presets and [[Forza Horizon 4/Forza Edition|Forza Edition cars]], which come with upgrade presets and a gameplay progression perk. Several special car variants such as Horizon Story cars are only playable in certain gameplay events.

Every unlockable car can be listed in and bought from the Auction House. Alternatively, a series of cars are featured in the Forzathon Shop for the duration of a Season, where they can be bought for a specific amount of Forzathon Points.

==Trivia==
*A large portion of the car list was leaked on June 23, 2018 by a reddit user after the title was mistakenly made available for download for users that have pre-ordered the title.<ref>{{ref|https://old.reddit.com/r/forza/comments/8t9sys/list_of_cars_showing_in_downloaded_folder_for_fh4/|List of cars showing in downloaded folder for FH4|reddit.com|06/24/2018}}</ref> It revealed the return of Barn Finds and Forza Edition cars, as well as the addition of Horizon Stories cars.
**Another portion of the car list was leaked on August 11, 2018 on gtplanet.net that included 'Bond' variant cars as notable additions.<ref>https://imgur.com/a/ym0ngMp (credits to TheAdmiester)</ref><ref>https://pastebin.com/8KS1deDN (credits to TheAdmiester)</ref>
*On September 7, 2018, an official car list featuring the majority of the more than 450 cars available to play at launch was released, which also revealed the absence of [[Mitsubishi]] cars in ''Forza Horizon 4''.<ref>{{ref|https://forzamotorsport.net/en-us/news/wir_9_7_18|Forza Week in Review 9-7-18|forzamotorsport.net|09-08-2018}}</ref>
**Later on the 15th of January 2019, Mitsubishi returned with the release of the Free Mitsubishi Motors Car Pack DLC.<ref>{{ref|https://www.gtplanet.net/mitsubishi-returns-to-forza-horizon-4-seven-free-cars-coming-january-15/|Mitsubishi Returns to Forza Horizon 4; Seven Free Cars Coming January 15|gtplanet.net|01-24-2018}}</ref>

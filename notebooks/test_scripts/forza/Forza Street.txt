'''Cars''' in ''[[Forza Street]]'' are categorized into four classes including Muscle, Sports, Super and Street. A total of 92 cars can currently be unlocked, with a further 3 cars currently known to be locked.

They are unlocked from cards and feature a [[Performance Index]] value, with a higher value referring to better performance. Players may add Upgrades to their car to improve its Performance Index.

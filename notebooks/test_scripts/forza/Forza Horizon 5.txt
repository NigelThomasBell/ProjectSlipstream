'''Cars''' in ''[[Forza Horizon 5]]'' can be purchased from the [[Autoshow]] and the [[Auction House]]. 

Some vehicles can only be won as [[Horizon Wheelspin|Wheelspin or Super Wheelspin]] rewards, as prizes from completing goals in the [[Forza Horizon 5/Festival Playlist|Festival Playlist]], by completing accolades, as [[:Category:Barn Find (FH5)|Barn Finds]], through the [[:Category:Car Mastery Reward (FH5)|Car Mastery]] skill trees of certain vehicles, as prizes through the [[:Category:Car Collector Reward (FH5)|Car Collector]], or as part of [[Forza Horizon 5/Downloadable Content|downloadable content]] packs.

Other vehicles may only be available during specific events, Showcases, or expeditions.

There are also special car variants called ''Forza Edition'' cars, which come with upgrade presets and a gameplay progression perk.

Every unlockable car can be viewed and bought from the Auction House. Alternatively, a selection of cars are listed in the Forzathon Shop for the duration of a season, where they can be bought for a specific amount of Forzathon Points.

Vehicles are assigned a rarity, with rarer vehicles including a greater number of perks for their Car Mastery: [[:Category:Common (FH5)|Common]], [[:Category:Rare (FH5)|Rare]], [[:Category:Epic (FH5)|Epic]], [[:Category:Legendary (FH5)|Legendary]], and [[:Category:Forza Edition (FH5)|Forza Edition]].

All vehicles have a Car Type assigned, which categorises similar vehicles together for events, PR stunts, and multiplayer:

'''Cars''' in ''[[Forza Horizon 3]]'' can be purchased from the Autoshow or [[Auction House]]. They are also rewarded to players from [[Horizon Wheelspin]]s, discovering [[Barn Find]]s, unlocking [[Forza Horizon 3/Skill Shop|Skill Shop perks]] and winning [[Forza Horizon 3/Forzathon|Forzathon events]]. Players may also increase their vehicle roster by purchasing [[Forza Horizon 3/Downloadable Content|downloadable content]].

Each car is categorised within a division that puts it against similar cars. The [[Performance Index]] determines the performance capabilities of a car, with D featuring the slowest and S2 the fastest cars. A car's type and performance index can apply certain event restrictions.

Unique ''[[Forza Horizon 3/Horizon Edition|Horizon Edition]]'' cars are equipped with unique gameplay perks that reward players a boost for skill points, experience points or credits whilst driving such vehicle. In addition, they are customised with performance upgrades and black license plates containing gold lettering.

The [[Horizon Promo]] feature allows players to receive a credit reward for photographing each car in the game including traffic vehicles.
